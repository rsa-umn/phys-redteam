# Events and Conferences
This page will serve as a collection of Events and Conferences related to Physical Red Teaming.

# ISACA Conference 2026
https://www.isaca.org/training-and-events/conferences/isaca-north-america-conference

To be held in Las Vegas and virtually this year. 

ISACA started in 1969 as an association for systems auditing and control, it
has now grown to encompass information technology governance, auditing, risk
management, and cybersecurity.

# RSA Conference 2026
https://www.rsaconference.com/

Started as a conference for customers of the RSA encryption algorithm awhile back, now it is a 
influential cybersecurity conference held around the world. From their website:

_As the cybersecurity industry’s convening authority, RSAC brings together diverse minds to 
exchange perspectives, knowledge, and ideas. RSAC provides the world’s leading platform for 
uniting and advancing the cybersecurity community to create a safer society. RSAC is at the 
cutting edge of cybersecurity innovation and education. The company’s flagship event, RSAC 
Conference, is the largest and most influential global gathering in cybersecurity. RSAC gives 
cybersecurity professionals a platform to connect and grow._
